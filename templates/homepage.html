Verda

Every forest starts with 1 acorn

The Problem

Every day humanity drains resources from the planet without even thinking about the inpacts on the nature. Many rivers are poluted, soil is being overused, animals are treated like objects and forest are being destroyed. And for what? So that we can use everyday luxuries like fur coats and animal bone decors.

The solution

We created a chatbot which helps the society to learn and understand the enviromental problems that are surrounding us. It will be able to analyze your questions and give you information about the enviromental topic you chose.

About the project

Verda is based on a chatbot from the 60s that mimics the speech pattern of a Rogerian psychotherapist. But we differ from the original by adding text to speech module and speech to text, so that you can talk to Verda.
